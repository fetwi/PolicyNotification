Introduction

The purpose of Policy Notification (PN)-87U is to update the authority limits for Construction and Architectural and Engineering Services.

Background

Temporary authority limits were provided for Construction and Architectural and Engineering Services in support of the Accelerated Infrastructure Program. These limits are in effect from April 1, 2009 to March 31, 2011. On April 1, 2011, new approval and signing authority limits will come into effect and will require new delegation forms for all positions requiring Construction and Architectural and Engineering Services authorities. Any new procurement or procurement in progress as of April 1, 2011 must use the new limits.

Effective Date

This PN is effective immediately.

Revisions to the Current Policy

Approval and Signing Authority Limits for Construction and Architectural and Engineering Services have been revised and replace the temporary limits put in place for the Accelerated Infrastructure Program. The revised limits are effective April 1, 2011. Annex A contains the changes to the Supply Manual.

Contact Person

For more information on the Supply Manual changes, contact Susan Blakeney, by phone at 819-956-8235, or by e-mail at: susan.blakeney@tpsgc-pwgsc.gc.ca.
