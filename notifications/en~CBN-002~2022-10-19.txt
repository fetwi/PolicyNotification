<h3>Purpose</h3>
<p>The purpose of CanadaBuys Notification (CBN) - 002 is to notify <abbr title="Public Services and Procurement Canada">PSPC</abbr> Buyers that Standard Instructions (SI) 2004ACB (AchatsCanadaBuys) for non-competitive requirements in CanadaBuys (SAP Ariba) are now available.</p>
<h3>Effective date</h3>
<p><abbr title="CanadaBuys Notification">CBN</abbr> - 002 is effective immediately.</p>
<p>The Standard Instructions (SI) 2004ACB are available in the Standard Acquisition Clauses and Conditions (SACC) Manual</p>
<h3>Application</h3>
<ul>
<li>Standard Instructions (SI) 2004ACB are for use in CanadaBuys (SAP Ariba).</li>
<li>All references in the solicitation, including hyperlinks, to the <abbr title="Standard Acquisition Clauses and Conditions Manual">SACC</abbr> Manual 2004 – Standard Instructions - Goods or Services – Non-Competitive Requirements must be removed and replaced with a reference to SI 2004ACB.</li>
</ul>
<h3>Revisions to the Supply Manual</h3>
<p>There are no revisions to the Supply Manual.</p>
<h3>Revisions to the Standard Acquisition Clauses and Conditions Manual (SACC)</h3>
<p>The <abbr title="Standard Acquisition Clauses and Conditions Manual">SACC</abbr> Manual has been revised in support of this <abbr title="CanadaBuys Notification">CBN</abbr>. For more details, refer to the Affected <abbr title="Standard Acquisition Clauses and Conditions Manual">SACC</abbr> Manual sections listed below.</p>
<h3>Revisions to the Standard Procurement Templates </h3>
<p>There are no revisions to the Standard Procurement Templates.</p>
<h3>Contact information</h3>
<p>For support on assembly of solicitation documents in CanadaBuys (SAP Ariba), <abbr title="Public Services and Procurement Canada">PSPC</abbr> Buyers are encouraged to contact the <a href="https://canadabuys.canada.ca/en/support" rel="external">CanadaBuys Service Desk</a> for assistance.</p>
<p>Enquiries related to <abbr title="CanadaBuys Notification">CBN</abbr> - 002 and CanadaBuys (SAP Ariba) can be sent by email to the CanadaBuys Service Desk at <a href="https://canadabuys.canada.ca/en/support/email-us" rel="external">Email us | CanadaBuys</a>.</p>
<p>Questions related to the <abbr title="Standard Acquisition Clauses and Conditions Manual">SACC</abbr> Manual can be sent by email to the following address: <a href="mailto:TPSGC.Outilsdapprovisionnement-ProcurementTools.PWGSC@tpsgc-pwgsc.gc.ca" rel="external">TPSGC.Outilsdapprovisionnement-ProcurementTools.PWGSC@tpsgc-pwgsc.gc.ca</a>.</p> </div><div class="field field-name-field-assoc-sm-items-view field-type-viewfield field-label-hidden">
