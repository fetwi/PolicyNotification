<p>1. Treasury Board Secretariate has provided notice of the amendment of previously established thresholds for the international trade agreements. The new thresholds are applicable for two years, January 1, 1998 to December 31, 1999.</p>
<p>2. Revised thresholds</p>

<li>Departments and Agencies</li>

</ol>

<p>Construction:<br/>

<p>Services:<br/>

					(b) CAN$ 363,300</p>
<p>Construction:<br/>
				(a) CAN$ 9,400,000<br/>
