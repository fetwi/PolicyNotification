<h2>Effective date</h2>
<p>Policy Notification 11R4 (PN-11R4) is effective immediately.</p>
<h2>Introduction</h2>
<p>PN-11R4 updates and enhances PN-11R3 promulgated on October 25, 2012 by: 
</p><ol type="a">
<li>providing a fixed period of three months for the completion of Vendor Performance Corrective Measure (VPCM) assessments; </li>
<li>providing guidance for the consistent application of <abbr title="Vendor Performance Corrective Measure">VPCM</abbr> assessment principles;</li>
<li>streamlining the governance process for review and approval of <abbr title="Vendor Performance Corrective Measure">VPCM</abbr>s; and</li>
<li>clarifying the procedures for applying historical information contained in the 
	Vendor Information Management System (VIM) to <abbr title="Vendor Performance Corrective Measure">VPCM</abbr> applicable cases. 
</li>
</ol>
<p></p>
<h2>Revisions to the <cite>Supply Manual</cite></h2>
<p>In support of this <abbr title="Policy Notification">PN</abbr>, changes have been made to the <cite>Supply Manual</cite>. Those sections are listed under Affected <cite>Supply Manual</cite> sections below.</p>
<h2>Revisions to the Standard Acquisition Clauses and Conditions (SACC) Manual</h2>
<p>No change has been made to the <abbr title="Standard Acquisition Clauses and Conditions">SACC</abbr>.</p>
<h2>ELF Documents</h2>
<p>The two following documents are available in the Electronic Forms (ELF) application:</p>
<ul>
<li>Direction for Adding Notes in <abbr title="Vendor Information Management System">VIM</abbr> and Authorization to Proceed with <abbr title="Vendor Performance Corrective Measure">VPCM</abbr> Assessment (Form 149) </li>
<li><abbr title="Vendor Performance Corrective Measure">VPCM</abbr> Assessment (Form 149-1) </li>
</ul>
<h2>Contact Information</h2>
<p>For more information, contact the VPCM co-ordinator by sending an e-mail to: <a href="mailto:CoordPMCRF.VPCMPCoord@tpsgc-pwgsc.gc.ca" rel="external">CoordPMCRF.VPCMPCoord@tpsgc-pwgsc.gc.ca</a>.</p>
