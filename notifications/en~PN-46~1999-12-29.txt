<h2 class="pn-content-title">Content of PN-46</h2>
<div class="field field-name-field-body field-type-text-long field-label-hidden">
<p>1. Treasury Board Secretariat has provided notice of the amendment of previously established thresholds for the international trade agreements. The new thresholds are applicable for two years, January 1, 2000 to December 31, 2001.</p>
