<h2 class="pn-content-title">Content of PN-103</h2>
<div class="field field-name-field-body field-type-text-long field-label-hidden">
<h3>Introduction</h3>
<p>The purpose of Policy Notification 103 (PN-103) is to update the instructions to contracting officers on the process to acquire goods and services when there is an emergency requirement.</p>
<h3>Effective Date</h3>
<p>This <abbr title="Policy Notification">PN</abbr> is effective immediately.</p>
<h3>Background</h3>
<p>This <abbr title="Policy Notification">PN</abbr> is being published to clarify the process that applies during an emergency situation.</p>
<h3>Revisions to Current Policy</h3>
<p>In addition to changes to existing sections of the Supply Manual, this <abbr title="Policy Notification">PN</abbr> introduces a new section on emergency requirements. Please refer to the "Affected Supply Manual sections" below to consult these sections.</p>
<h3>Contact</h3>
