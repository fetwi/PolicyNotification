<h2>Purpose</h2>
<p>The purpose of Policy Notification 91R1 (PN-91R1) is to provide updated guidance on the debriefing process.</p>
<h2>Effective date</h2>
<p><abbr title="Policy Notification">PN</abbr>-91R1 and the related changes to the <cite>Supply Manual</cite>, <abbr title="Standard Acquisition Clauses and Conditions">SACC</abbr> Manual and Standard Procurement Templates are effective immediately.</p>
<h2>Policy</h2>
<p>In addition to other changes, this <abbr title="Policy Notification">PN</abbr> includes specific direction on providing a feedback session instead of a debriefing when a solicitation is cancelled and reissued. When a solicitation is cancelled and reissued, it is very important that contracting officers provide the same information to all bidders/offerors/suppliers, in a generic format, to ensure fairness, openness and transparency throughout the procurement process.</p>
<h2>Revisions to the <cite>Supply Manual</cite></h2>
<p>In support of this <abbr title="Policy Notification">PN</abbr>, the <cite>Supply Manual</cite> has been updated. To view the modified sections, please refer to <a href="#PN-91R1affected_supply_manual_sections">Affected <cite>Supply Manual</cite> Section(s)</a> below.</p>
<h2>Revisions to the Standard Acquisition Clauses and Conditions (SACC) Manual</h2>
<p>Changes have been made to the <abbr title="Standard Acquisition Clauses and Conditions">SACC</abbr>. To view the modified clauses, please refer to <a href="#PN-91R1affected_sacc_manual_sections">Affected <abbr title="Standard Acquisition Clauses and Conditions">SACC</abbr> Manual Section(s)</a> below. Contracting officers are to incorporate these changes into their procurement documents.</p>
<h2>Revisions to the Standard Procurement Templates</h2>
<p>The following <a href="https://canadabuys.canada.ca/en/how-procurement-works/policies-and-guidelines/standard-acquisition-clauses-and-conditions-manual#TEMPLATES">standard procurement templates</a> have been revised. Contracting officers are to incorporate these changes in their procurement documents.</p>
<ul>
<li>February 2013 Low Dollar Value Bid Solicitation and Resulting Contract Template (Simple)</li>
<li>February 2013 Medium Complexity Bid Solicitation and Resulting Contract Template (MC)</li>
<li>February 2013 Higher Complexity Bid Solicitation and Resulting Contract Template (HC)</li>
<li>February 2013 Request for Standing Offers Template (RFSO)</li>
<li>February 2013 Request for Supply Arrangements Template (RFSA)</li>
</ul>
