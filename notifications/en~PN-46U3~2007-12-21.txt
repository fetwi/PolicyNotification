<h3 class="pn-content-title">Content of PN-46U3</h3>
<div class="field field-name-field-body field-type-text-long field-label-hidden">
<ol>
<li>Treasury Board Secretariat has provided notice (Contracting Policy Notice 2007-05) of the amendment of previously established thresholds for the international trade agreements. The new thresholds in Canadian dollars are applicable for two years, from January 1, 2008, to December 31, 2009.</li>
<li>
<p>Revised thresholds:</p>
<p><strong>Note: Based on the strength of the Canadian dollar, there has been a decrease in the trade thresholds.</strong></p>
</li>
</ol>
