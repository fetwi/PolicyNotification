<h2>Purpose</h2>
<p>The purpose of CanadaBuys Notification (CBN) – 001 is to notify <abbr title="Public Services and Procurement Canada">PSPC</abbr> Buyers of updated standard instructions for use in CanadaBuys (SAP Ariba). </p>
<p>The updated Standard Instructions (SI) 2003ACB are provided to <abbr title="Public Services and Procurement Canada">PSPC</abbr> Buyers for use only in procurements in CanadaBuys (SAP Ariba).</p>
<p>The updated Standard Instructions (SI) 2003ACB replace in their entirety any previous version of the <abbr title="Standard Instructions">SI</abbr> 2003ACB.</p>
<h2>Effective date</h2>
<p><abbr title="CanadaBuys Notification">CBN</abbr> - 001 is effective immediately.</p>
<p>The Standard Instructions (SI) 2003ACB are available in the <abbr title="Standard Acquisition Clauses and Conditions">SACC</abbr> Manual. </p>
<h2>Application</h2>
<ul>
<li>Standard Instructions (SI) 2003ACB are for the exclusive use in CanadaBuys (SAP Ariba).</li>
<li>Bids must be submitted through CanadaBuys (SAP Ariba).</li>
<li><abbr title="Standard Instructions">SI</abbr> 2003ACB should not be used for forms of bid submission and receipt other than through CanadaBuys (SAP Ariba). </li>
</ul>
<h2>Background</h2>
<p>In February 2022, the Standard Instructions (SI) 2003ACB (AchatsCanadaBuys) for competitive requirements in SAP Ariba, were made available for early access through the CanadaBuys Service Desk. <abbr title="Public Services and Procurement Canada">PSPC</abbr> Buyers had to contact the <a href="https://canadabuys.canada.ca/en/support" rel="external">CanadaBuys Service Desk</a> to request access to the <abbr title="Standard Instructions">SI</abbr> 2003ACB.</p>
<p><abbr title="CanadaBuys Notification">CBN</abbr> – 001 now provides updated <abbr title="Standard Instructions">SI</abbr> 2003ACB for use by <abbr title="Public Services and Procurement Canada">PSPC</abbr> Buyers for procurements in CanadaBuys (SAP Ariba).</p>
<h2>Requirements and responsibility</h2>
<p>The updated <abbr title="Standard Instructions">SI</abbr> 2003ACB have been published in the <abbr title="Standard Acquisition Clauses and Conditions">SACC</abbr> Manual.</p>
<p>It is the responsibility of the <abbr title="Public Services and Procurement Canada">PSPC</abbr> Buyer to ensure the <abbr title="Standard Instructions">SI</abbr> 2003ACB is only used in procurements in CanadaBuys (SAP Ariba).</p>
<h2>Guidance</h2>
<p>CONSIDERATIONS FOR DEVELOPING A SOLICITATION IN CANADABUYS (SAP ARIBA)</p>
<ol>
<li><p>Buyers must only include the reference to <abbr title="Standard Instructions">SI</abbr> 2003ACB for procurements in CanadaBuys (SAP Ariba).</p>
<p>NOTE: All references including hyperlinks to the <a href="https://canadabuys.canada.ca/en/how-procurement-works/policies-and-guidelines/standard-acquisition-clauses-and-conditions-manual#2003"><abbr title="Standard Acquisition Clauses and Conditions">SACC</abbr> Manual 2003 – Standard Instructions - Goods or Services - Competitive Requirements</a> must be removed in the solicitation document, and replaced with a reference to the <a href="https://canadabuys.canada.ca/en/how-procurement-works/policies-and-guidelines/standard-acquisition-clauses-and-conditions-manual#2003ACB"><abbr title="Standard Instructions">SI</abbr> 2003ACB in the <abbr title="Standard Acquisition Clauses and Conditions">SACC</abbr> Manual</a>.</p>
</li>
<li>Buyers must indicate that bids will only be accepted through CanadaBuys (SAP Ariba) in the solicitation documents. </li>
<li><p>Note the following text for consideration, as applicable (this is not an exhaustive list of solicitation wording to reflect the use of <abbr title="Standard Instructions">SI</abbr> 2003ACB).</p>
<div>
<p><strong>PART 2 – BIDDERS INSTRUCTIONS</strong></p>
<p><strong>Section 2.2 Submission of Bids</strong><br/>
                “Bids must only be submitted <strong>through CanadaBuys (SAP Ariba)</strong> by the date and time indicated in the bid solicitation. All bids submitted through other means will neither be accepted nor considered.”</p>
<p><strong>Section 2.4 Enquiries - Bid Solicitation</strong><br/>
                “All enquiries must be submitted in writing to the Contracting Authority no later than <span>__________ <kbd>(insert number of days)</kbd></span> calendar days before the bid closing date <strong>by using the Event Messages tab within CanadaBuys (SAP Ariba)</strong>. Enquiries received after that time may not be answered.”</p>
<p><strong>Section 2.6 Improvement of Requirement During Solicitation Period</strong><br/>
                “Should bidders consider that the specifications or Statement of Work contained in the bid solicitation could be improved technically or technologically, bidders are invited to make suggestions, in writing, <strong>through the Event Messages tab within CanadaBuys (SAP Ariba)</strong>, to the Contracting Authority named in the bid solicitation. Bidders must clearly outline the suggested improvement as well as the reason for the suggestion. </p>
<p>Suggestions that do not restrict the level of competition nor favour a particular bidder will be given consideration provided they are submitted to the Contracting Authority at least <span>__________</span> days before the bid closing date. Canada will have the right to accept or reject any or all suggestions.”</p>
<p><strong>PART 3 – BID PREPARATION INTRUCTIONS</strong></p>
<p><strong>Section 3.1 Bid Preparation Instructions</strong><br/>
                “Canada requests that the Bidder submits its bid in accordance with <strong>section 05 of the 2003ACB</strong> Standard Instructions.”</p>
</div>
</li>
</ol>
