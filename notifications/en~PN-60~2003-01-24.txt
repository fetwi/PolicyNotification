<h3>A0012T Communications - Solicitation Period</h3>
<p>This Policy Notification is being released to advise contracting officers of recent changes done to clause A0012T (<a href="#PN-60annex-a">Annex A</a>) which deals with enquiries, and other communications, regarding the bid solicitation, from the issue date of the solicitation up to the closing date.</p>
<h3>A0000T Standard Instructions and Conditions</h3>
<p>Clause A0000T (<a href="#PN-60annex-b">Annex B</a>), paragraph 1. (b) was revised by deleting the reference to "Part B", so that the clause can be used both in solicitations and contracts. </p>
