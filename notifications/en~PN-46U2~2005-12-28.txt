<h2 class="pn-content-title">Content of PN-46U2</h2>
<div>
<div class="field field-name-field-body field-type-text-long field-label-hidden">
<ol>
<li>Treasury Board Secretariat has provided notice (CPN 2005-3)<br/>
						of the amendment of previously established thresholds for the<br/>
						international trade agreements. The new thresholds in Canadian<br/>
						dollars are applicable for two years, from January 1, 2006 to<br/>
						December 31, 2007.
</li>
<li>Revised thresholds:</li>
</ol>
<blockquote><p>Note:	Based on the strength of the Canadian dollar, there has been a decrease in the trade thresholds. </p>
</blockquote>
<h3>World Trade Organization Agreement on Government Procurement  (<acronym title="World Trade Organization - Agreement on Government Procurement">WTO-AGP</acronym>)</h3>
<p><strong>Goods:</strong> Departments and Agencies - C$245,000<br/>
<strong>Services:</strong> Departments and Agencies - C$245,000<br/>
<strong>Construction:</strong> Departments and Agencies - C$9,400,000</p>
<h3>North American Free Trade Agreement (NAFTA)</h3>
<dl>
<dt><strong>Goods:</strong></dt>
<dd>Departments and Agencies - C$32,400</dd>
<dd>Enterprises (Crown Corporations) - C$420,000</dd>
<dt><strong>Services:</strong></dt>
<dd>Departments and Agencies - C$84,000</dd>
<dd>Enterprises (Crown Corporations) - C$420,000</dd>
<dt><strong>Construction:</strong></dt>
<dd>Departments and Agencies - C$10,900,000</dd>
<dd>Enterprises (Crown Corporations) - C$13,400,000</dd>
</dl>
<p>The thresholds for the Agreement on Internal Trade remain unchanged at $25,000 for goods and $100,000 for services and construction.</p>
<h3>Changes to Supply Manual</h3>
<p>These changes will be reflected in next amendment of the Supply Manual, expected to be released in June 2006.</p>
